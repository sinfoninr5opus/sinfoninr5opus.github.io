What is the Millennial Starter Pack?

The Millennial Starter Pack is a party card game for the self-deprecating soul. It's a Millennial-themed fill-in-the-blank game with unique interaction cards designed for unexpected twists and replayability. We've meticulously curated the content for just the right amount feels and relevance before it gets downright depressing. Even then, it is said that no amount of drinking can wash away what this deck holds for you...

The game was created by three friends who wanted to stay in touch with each other across the world. Many friendships have endured (and thankfully survived) the early play-tests, so we put together this site to bring this experience to you!

Being a Millennial is a collective experience, so please help us build this game, and evolve it with us because, at the end of the day, this is a game about all you millennials out there.

 

Something for every Snowflake.

The Basic Starter Pack includes 179 cards that are anecdotally proven to provide hours of entertainment, schadenfreude, and self-loathing. Want more existential crises? We have 304 additional cards crammed into the Expansion Starter Pack for that.
